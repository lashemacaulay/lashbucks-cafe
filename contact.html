Opportunity for franchise

Please send your proposal to yourstore@starbucks.com and include your current business details, contact details and proposed geographical area. As we are only developing specific geographical areas at this time, we will not be able to respond to you individually. However, we will retain your contact details on file and if appropriate, will contact you in due course.
